# Customer Segmentation & Business Intelligence Platform

Shopper Spectrum is an end-to-end customer analytics platform built using **Python**, **Machine Learning**, **RFM Analysis**, and **Streamlit**.

The platform helps businesses identify valuable customers, detect churn risk, discover product relationships, and generate actionable business insights through interactive dashboards.

# Business Problem

Businesses often possess large amounts of transactional data but struggle to answer questions such as:

- Who are our most valuable customers?
- Which customers are likely to churn?
- Which products should be recommended together?
- Which countries generate the highest revenue?
- How can marketing campaigns be personalized?

Shopper Spectrum addresses these challenges using customer segmentation, recommendation systems, and business analytics.
